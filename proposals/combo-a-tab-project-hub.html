Why this combination works
Arch fix The tab bar means Create, Stash, and Stats are panels on the home page — they are no longer separate HTML pages acting as landing pages. index.html, manager.html, and stitch.html only open once a project is actively chosen.
Projects first The Projects tab is the default and the active project card is the first thing you see — no separate hero section, no greeting section, no ResumeCard floating above the list. One card, full context, immediate actions.
Choice per card Every project row has both Track and Edit buttons. The old design silently sent you to the Tracker; now you decide.
No duplication The QuickTiles "Open Tracker" and "Open Stash Manager" tiles are gone — those destinations are now the Track buttons on each card and the Stash tab respectively. Nothing appears twice.
