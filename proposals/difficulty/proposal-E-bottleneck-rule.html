Proposal E — Bottleneck Rule

Model E

Each factor scores independently. A weighted average gives the "composite" score, BUT the overall tier cannot fall below the tier of the single hardest factor (the bottleneck). The principle: one extreme dimension cannot be outweighed by easy dimensions. A pattern that is Beginner in everything except extreme confetti cannot be rated below Intermediate — the confetti sets a floor. Shows both the composite and the bottleneck factor prominently.
