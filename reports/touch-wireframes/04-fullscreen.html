04 — Focus / full-screen mode

iPad landscape (1024×768)

Tablet — chrome hidden, floating mini-bar bottom-center, Exit always visible

Phone (375×812)

Phone — same pattern, mini-bar fills width with Exit on the right

Focus mode. Hides every chrome surface (top bar, top toolbar, side panels, status bar). The floating mini-bar at bottom-center has the absolute essentials: active tool, active colour, undo, zoom −/+, and a permanent ✕ Exit button. The Exit button never auto-fades; the rest can fade to 30 % after 4 s of inactivity (configurable, defaults: phone on, tablet off). Entry: toolbar button, F key, or long-press → Focus mode. Exit: the button, Esc, or F again.
