03 — Lpanel three-state design

iPad landscape (1024×768)

Hidden — canvas gets full width; tap ≡ to reveal
Rail — 56 px; active colour always visible; tap › to expand
Open — 320 px overlay (current behaviour); tap ‹ to go to rail

iPad portrait (768×1024)

Hidden (default on portrait) — left edge swipe reveals rail
Open — bottom-sheet (current behaviour) with 70 dvh max

Phone (375×812)

Phone — hidden by default; tap ≡ for bottom-sheet

Three states. Hidden gives the canvas the full screen; rail shows the always-needed active colour and legend jump in 56 px; open is the existing 320 px overlay (desktop) or bottom-sheet (mobile/tablet). The hamburger cycles hidden → rail → open → hidden. State persists per viewport class so the user gets sensible defaults when switching devices.
