05 — Toolbar improvements (before / after)

Tablet (768 px wide)

BEFORE — top toolbar, dense, ~22 px chips
AFTER — bottom-fixed toolbar, 44 px chips, Hand + colour swatch + Focus in overflow

Phone (375 px wide)

BEFORE — top toolbar wraps to 2+ rows; tools in hardest-reach zone
AFTER — minimal top bar, bottom-fixed toolbar with most-used tools

Toolbar AFTER hits the 44 px floor everywhere; gaps go from 4 px to 8 px; the toolbar lives at the bottom on touch viewports (matching the existing tracker action bar pattern); the new Hand tool is permanently visible; an active-colour swatch sits inline so the user always knows what they'll paint with; Focus moves into either the visible row or the overflow.
