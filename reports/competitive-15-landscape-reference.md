## Competitor Quick-Reference Cards

### StitchMate
- **Founded:** Active 2024–2026; ranked #1 by LordLibidan April 2026
- **Pricing:** Free trial → from £1.60/pattern; passes from £47.99/yr
- **Differentiators:** FLOW Score (stitchability metric 0-100); ConfettiScope overlay (claims 79% fewer confetti vs Stitch Fiddle); 50+ brands; 48-font text tool; PK-certified badge
- **Weaknesses:** Paid per-pattern; no integrated tracking or stash
- **Key lesson for us:** FLOW Score is the most important missing metric in our tool; it names and quantifies what users already complain about

### Stitch Fiddle
- **Pricing:** Free tier + Premium ~$5.50/mo
- **Differentiators:** Largest free grid (2000×2000); multi-craft; most accessible free tool
- **Weaknesses:** No PK compatibility; selection + backstitch require Premium; confetti rate is among the worst in market (15.8% in benchmarks)
- **Key lesson for us:** Being free and accessible wins discovery; confetti rate is a genuine quality gap they haven't fixed

### Thread-Bare
- **Pricing:** $10 per pattern (download-based)
- **Differentiators:** Best-in-class dithering (cited "first class" by LordLibidan); monthly updates
- **Weaknesses:** Paid; no tracking, no stash
- **Key lesson for us:** Dithering quality matters to power users; worth investing in our dithering options

### Pattern Keeper
- **Platform:** Android only; $10.50 (2-week trial)
- **Differentiators:** 100k+ reviews; works with any PDF; parking method tracking (claimed unique); combines multi-page patterns
- **Weaknesses:** Android only; no creation; requires a PDF (our format must export correctly)
- **Key lesson for us:** We should explicitly position ourselves as "make the pattern that goes into Pattern Keeper"

### Cross Stitch Paradise
- **Platform:** Android, free
- **Differentiators:** Opens XSD files; 6500+ reviews; tracks full/half/quarter/backstitch
- **Weaknesses:** No analytics; no creation; session tracking but no insights
- **Key lesson for us:** The free tracking market is served; our advantage must be the integrated loop

### WinStitch / MacStitch
- **Platform:** Desktop (Win + Mac)
- **Pricing:** ~$52 one-time
- **Differentiators:** 30 brands; opens PCStitch files; yearly updates; professional PDF output
- **Weaknesses:** Desktop-only; expensive; steep learning curve; no stash, no tracking
- **Key lesson for us:** Desktop tools are retreating; web+PWA is winning
